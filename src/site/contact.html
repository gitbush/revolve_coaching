Frequently asked questions

how did you get started as a life coach?

Looking back now I can see that a personal disaster was absolutely the trigger point for me radically changing my life for the better. I knew I wanted to serve people like me and I wanted to do it in the most positive way possible….so I did a diploma in life coaching with the Coaching Academy.

The difference between your main 3 offerings?

The main differences are length of program, frequency of sessions and depth of discovery therefore change.
Swift: short, frequent and broad.
Signature: medium, every 2 weeks and extensive.
Shift: Long, every 3 weeks and total.

WOULD YOU TELL ME IF COACHING WASN’T RIGHT FOR ME?

Of cuorse. I make it a priority to only take on clients for whom coaching is right and I am a good fit for. That's why the initial Clarity call is so important. Its free, no obligation and within that we explore your needs and our chemistry.
